Our Story

We are a photography and modeling agency based in Thika, Kenya. We are open from Monday to Sunday including Holidays. We are currently scouting, developing and home to 10 models working locally and setting eyes on the global market.

Glitch Cloud was founded in June 2019, by Brendan Kiarie. We have worked with over 500 clients across the country. Our self-critical approach and ensuring we execute above and beyond client expectations has earned us trust, support, referrals and long-term works from all our clients.

We are proud of the exposure, quality of work, and clientele that we continue to book. We are excited and appreciate all our clients whole heartedly.
